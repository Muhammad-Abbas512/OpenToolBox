Our Story

From a simple idea to a global platform serving millions

OpenToolBox was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to make digital tools accessible to everyone, everywhere. What started as a small collection of PDF utilities has grown into a comprehensive platform offering dozens of free tools for PDF processing, image editing, SEO optimization, and more.

Our founder, Muhammad Abbas, was frustrated by the complexity and cost of existing online tools. He believed that powerful digital utilities should be free, easy to use, and available to anyone with an internet connection. This vision drove him to create OpenToolBox.

Today, OpenToolBox serves over 5 million users across 150+ countries. We've processed more than 100 million files and continue to expand our toolset based on user feedback and emerging digital needs. Our commitment to privacy, security, and user experience remains at the core of everything we do.

We're proud to be a completely free platform, supported by ethical advertising and user donations. Our team works tirelessly to improve existing tools and create new ones that solve real-world problems.
