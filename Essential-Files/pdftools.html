2. PDF Compression: Reduce File Size by 80%

Email servers limit attachments to 10-25MB, cloud storage costs add up, and slow-loading PDFs frustrate recipients. Our PDF Compressor uses advanced optimization algorithms to reduce file size by up to 80% while maintaining visual fidelity.

How PDF Compression Works

PDF compression involves multiple techniques: image downsampling (reducing DPI), font subsetting (embedding only used characters), metadata stripping, and object stream optimization. Our tool automatically selects the optimal compression level based on your PDF content type - text-heavy documents compress more efficiently than image-rich brochures.

Compression Settings Explained

  • Maximum Compression: Reduces size by 70-80%. Ideal for email attachments, document archives, and internal sharing. Uses JPEG2000 compression for images at 72 DPI.
  • Balanced Compression: Reduces size by 50-60%. Perfect balance between quality and file size. Recommended for most users.
  • Lossless Compression: Reduces size by 20-30%. Zero quality loss. Best for legal documents, contracts, and printing.
üìä Benchmark 2026: A 50MB scanned magazine compressed to 9.4MB with Maximum setting - 81.2% reduction. Visual quality remained acceptable for on-screen reading. Text was 100% readable.

7. Optimize PDFs for Web & Print

Different use cases require different PDF optimizations. Our suite includes specialized optimization presets.

Web Optimization

Linearize PDFs for "byte serving" - enables fast streaming of PDFs in browsers. Viewers can read page 1 while page 10 still downloads. Essential for websites and digital publications.

Print Optimization

Convert RGB to CMYK color space for professional printing. Embed all fonts, increase image resolution to 300 DPI, and add printer marks. Prepares your PDF for commercial printing presses.

PDF/A Archiving

Convert standard PDFs to PDF/A format - the ISO-standardized version for long-term digital preservation. Ensures your documents remain readable for decades, regardless of software evolution.
